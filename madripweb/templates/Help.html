Our Offered Services

Following are the services which MADRIP provides.

Defective Area Detection

The effected area of the retina scan can be detected, and system can highlight all the effected areas extracted from the retina scan.

Identification of the stage

With the help of MADRIP youy can easily identify the stage of diabetic retinopathy, our system will tell the stage of diabetic retinopathy by scanning the retina scan, once the effected areas are highlighted.

MultiDimensional Analysis

You can have a clear insight of the specific retina scan, which includes the trends of the stage of diabetic retinopathy, some facts about the result you get with some attributes associated with it.

Detailed Report

You can have a detailed report of the retina scan which includes all the information regarding to that scan at one place.
